# Business / Agency Provider Features (LocalPro)

## 1. Organization & Company Profile

This establishes the **provider as a company rather than a single worker**.

**Features**

- Company profile page

- Business registration details

- Business permits upload

- Service categories offered

- Coverage areas (cities/provinces)

- Business operating hours

- Company branding (logo, banner)

- Portfolio / project gallery

- Insurance / certification display

- Company description

**Purpose**

- Build **trust and legitimacy**

- Attract **B2B clients**

***


# 2. Team & Staff Management

Agencies need to **manage multiple workers under one account**.

**Features**

- Add staff members

- Staff profile management

- Staff skill tagging

- Assign service categories per staff

- Staff availability calendar

- Staff verification

- Staff role assignment

**Staff Roles**

- Admin

- Dispatcher

- Worker/Technician

- Finance/Accounting

- Customer Support

**Example**\
Cleaning agency:

    Agency Owner
       |
       |-- Dispatcher
       |-- Cleaner 1
       |-- Cleaner 2
       |-- Cleaner 3

***


# 3. Job Assignment & Dispatch System

Instead of accepting jobs personally, **agencies assign jobs to workers**.

**Features**

- Job queue dashboard

- Assign worker to job

- Automatic worker matching

- Worker availability check

- Job routing

- Reassign worker

- Multi-worker assignment

- Route optimization (optional)

**Example Workflow**

Client books cleaning\
↓\
Agency receives job\
↓\
Dispatcher assigns Cleaner #3\
↓\
Cleaner receives job notification

***

# 6. Financial Management

Agencies must **manage income across multiple staff**.

**Features**

- Earnings dashboard

- Job revenue breakdown

- Commission breakdown

- Invoice generator

- Expense tracking

- Withdrawal requests

- Escrow status

**Example Earnings Split**

    Client pays: 3,000

    LocalPro commission: 15% = 450
    Agency receives: 2,550

    Agency pays worker: 1,500
