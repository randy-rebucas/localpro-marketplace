## Email Outreach Templates

### Template 1: White-Label Cold Outreach (Discovery Call Request)

**Subject Line Options:**
- "Let's explore white-label partnership for [Company Name]"
- "Franchise expansion opportunity: LocalPro platform integration"
- "[City] staffing platform—franchise partner inquiry"

**Email Body (145 words):**

```
Subject: Let's explore white-label partnership for [Company Name]

Hi [First Name],

I've been following [Company Name]'s expansion across [regions/cities], and I think LocalPro 
could be a natural fit for your growth strategy.

We power 100K+ verified service professionals across the Philippines, handling 10M+ placements 
annually. Our white-label platform lets you rebrand and resell staffing services to your 
customers—with revenue sharing starting at 60/40, scaling to 50/50+ at higher volumes.

For a company like [Company Name], this typically generates ₱[estimated annual volume]M in 
additional commission revenue within 12 months, while improving your customer retention through 
expanded service offerings.

Would you be available for a 20-minute call next week to explore how white-label could work 
specifically for [Company Name]'s footprint? I can share case studies from similar partners.

Best regards,
[Your Name]
LocalPro Partnership Team
[Phone] | [Email]
```

**Personalization Checklist:**
- [ ] Research company's current service footprint
- [ ] Identify expansion plans (press releases, investor updates)
- [ ] Calculate realistic annual volume estimate
- [ ] Reference specific city/region they operate in
- [ ] Use first name (not title) in greeting

**Subject Line:**

**Email Body (160 words):**

```

[Company Name]'s [service line]. He thought LocalPro's white-label staffing platform could

we're helping companies like [Competitor/Similar Company] add staffing-as-a-service to their 
offerings. Partners typically earn ₱[estimated range]M annually through commission sharing.

The integration is straightforward—usually 4-6 weeks from contract to pilot launch. And since 
[Referrer] knows us, he can vouch for the quality of support.

Would you have 15 minutes next Tuesday or Wednesday for a quick call? I can walk you through

### Template 4: PESO/LGU Formal Outreach (Government Partnership)

**Subject Line:**
- "DOLE-aligned workforce platform partnership for [LGU Name]"
- "Employment formalization opportunity for [LGU Name] PESO"
- "Memorandum of Agreement discussion—LocalPro PESO Program"

**Email Body (170 words):**

```
Subject: DOLE-aligned workforce platform partnership for [LGU Name]

Dear [PESO Director Name] / [Mayor/Official Title],

The LocalPro team is reaching out regarding a strategic partnership opportunity that aligns 
with [LGU Name]'s employment policy objectives and DOLE workforce formalization targets.

LocalPro operates the largest verified service professional network in the Philippines (100K+ 
providers, 10M+ annual placements). We're partnering directly with DOLE/TESDA-aligned LGUs 
to create digital infrastructure for:

• Centralized job matching for PESO jobseekers
• Wage formalization tracking (monthly reporting to DOLE)
• Skills certification pathways aligned with TESDA standards
• Economic data insights for LGU planning and DTI coordination

We're exploring a 12-week pilot structure (starting [month]) with [target region] PESO 
focusing on formalization of [priority sectors: construction, logistics, etc.].

The pilot requires no upfront investment from the LGU—LocalPro covers all technology costs 
in exchange for revenue sharing on successful placements.

Would your office be available for a 30-minute discussion to review the pilot framework 
and DOLE alignment structure? I can travel to [City] on [suggested dates].

Respectfully yours,

[Your Name]
Government Relations, LocalPro
[Phone] | [Email]
LocalPro Corporation
```

**Formal Language Elements:**
- Addresses by full title + surname
- Emphasizes government agency alignment (DOLE, TESDA, DICT)
- Uses "Respectfully yours" closing (government norm)
- Proposes travel to their location (respect for their position)
- Emphasizes "no upfront cost" (government concern)
- References "pilot framework" + "MOA" terminology

## Implementation Checklist

### Before Sending Any Outreach:

**White-Label Templates:**
- [ ] Research company website, news, LinkedIn (recent growth signals)
- [ ] Identify decision maker + verify correct spelling of name
- [ ] Estimate realistic annual volume based on footprint
- [ ] Personalize all bracketed fields
- [ ] Reference specific city/region they operate in
- [ ] Add case study example of similar company

**PESO/LGU Templates:**
- [ ] Confirm PESO Director name + correct spelling
- [ ] Identify Sanggunian committee responsible for employment
- [ ] Research LGU's current DOLE alignment status
- [ ] Note any local employment initiatives they're running
- [ ] Verify pilot dates don't conflict with LGU budget cycle

**Email Tracking:**

## Tone & Formatting Standards

### ✅ Do

- ✓ Use recipient's first name (shows personalization effort)
- ✓ Keep sentences short (max 15 words per sentence)
- ✓ Use concrete numbers and timeframes
- ✓ Lead with recipient's benefit, not LocalPro features
- ✓ Include specific next-step CTA
- ✓ Reference something specific about their company/situation
- ✓ Use contractions ("we're," "I've") for warmth

### ❌ Don't

- ✗ Start with "I wanted to reach out" (passive, overused)
- ✗ Use corporate jargon ("synergy," "leverage," "dynamic")
- ✗ Make it about LocalPro features first (lead with their pain)
- ✗ Run-on sentences or multiple paragraphs without breaks
- ✗ Use ALL CAPS for emphasis (looks aggressive)
- ✗ Generic greetings ("To Whom It May Concern")
- ✗ Unclear CTAs ("Let's chat sometime")
