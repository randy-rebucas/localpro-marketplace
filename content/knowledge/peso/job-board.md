## PESO job postings

Your office can post jobs directly on the LocalPro marketplace under the PESO office's profile. These jobs are tagged as **Government / LGU-Funded** and are visible to all providers in your coverage area (and optionally to the public).

PESO-posted jobs are used for:

- LGU maintenance and public works contracts awarded to livelihood program beneficiaries
- Community service projects (barangay clean-ups, repairs, landscaping)
- Government-sponsored skills training with paid practicum components
- TUPAD (Tulong Panghanapbuhay sa Ating Disadvantaged/Displaced) task contracts

## Posting a PESO job

1. Go to **PESO Portal → Jobs → Post a Job**
2. Fill in the job details:
   - **Title and description** — be specific about the task, location, and expected output
   - **Category** — service trade most relevant to the work
   - **Location** — the barangay or address where work will be performed
   - **Budget / compensation** — can be a fixed amount or daily rate × number of days
   - **Program tag** — link the job to a livelihood or TUPAD batch for report tracking
3. Set **Visibility**: Public (all providers in area) or Restricted (only workers in a specific livelihood group)
4. Click **Post**

The job goes live immediately — no quote bidding process is required. You can directly **Assign** it to one or more workers from your registry without waiting for bids, which is the standard workflow for funded programs.

## Directly assigning a job to workers

For TUPAD and livelihood contracts, you typically know in advance which workers will perform the tasks:

1. After posting, click **Assign Worker**
2. Search your registry by name, skill, or group
3. Select one or more workers
4. Confirm — each assigned worker is notified via SMS and in-app

Directly assigned jobs skip the quote and acceptance stage and go straight to **In Progress** status.

## Emergency broadcasts

The **Emergency Broadcast** feature sends an urgent SMS and push notification to all active providers in your office's coverage area — not just your enrolled workers. Use it for:

- Sudden calamity response (typhoon clean-up, flood relief)
- Critical infrastructure repair that needs immediate mobilisation
- Large-scale emergency employment programs activated by LGU executive order

To send an emergency broadcast:

1. Go to **Jobs → Emergency Broadcast**
2. Write a brief message (max 160 characters for SMS)
3. Select the affected area (barangay, city, or province-wide)
4. Optionally attach a link to a pre-posted job
5. Click **Broadcast**

> **Use this feature responsibly.** Emergency broadcasts interrupt providers and are logged for audit. Non-emergency use may result in restrictions on your office's account.

## Managing posted jobs

All PESO jobs are visible in **Jobs → All Jobs**, filterable by status, program, and date. From the jobs list you can:

- View applicants (for open-bid jobs)
- Reassign workers
- Mark jobs as completed and release payment (if applicable)
- Download a summary per job for program documentation

## Completion and payment

For LGU-funded jobs, payment is processed outside LocalPro (direct LGU payroll). In this case, mark the job as **Administratively Completed** — this closes the job in the system and records the outcome for reporting, without triggering an escrow release.

For market-rate jobs funded through LocalPro escrow (where the LGU pre-loads funds), payment flows through the standard escrow release process.
