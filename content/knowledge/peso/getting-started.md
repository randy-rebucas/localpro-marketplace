## What is the PESO Portal?

The **PESO Portal** is a dedicated section of LocalPro for Public Employment Service Offices (PESOs) — government employment units attached to city, municipal, or provincial LGUs. Through the portal, PESO officers can:

- Refer and onboard local workers to the LocalPro marketplace
- Verify and certify providers under livelihood programs
- Post government-sponsored or LGU-funded job listings
- Broadcast emergency employment opportunities
- Track employment outcomes and generate mandated reports

LocalPro's PESO integration supports the mandate of RA 8759 (PESO Act) by connecting the public employment service network with the digital service economy.

## Setting up your office

To create a PESO office account:

1. **Contact LocalPro** — PESO offices are not self-registered. Email **peso@localpro.ph** with your office name, LGU, and your official PESO designation letter.
2. **Account creation** — The LocalPro team creates your office record and sends you a Head Officer invitation.
3. **Complete your office profile** — Log in, upload your office logo, and confirm your contact details.
4. **Add staff officers** — Invite additional PESO staff who will share access to the portal.

## Office types

Your office is classified by LGU level:

| Type | Coverage |
|------|---------|
| **Provincial** | All municipalities and cities in the province |
| **City** | City proper and barangays under the city |
| **Municipal** | Municipal proper and constituent barangays |

The classification determines how your data appears in regional reports and in provider search results (workers you onboard are tagged with your LGU).

## Quick start checklist

- [ ] Complete your office profile (name, logo, address, LGU classification)
- [ ] Add at least one staff officer
- [ ] Enroll your first 5 workers using the referral or bulk onboarding flow
- [ ] Post one PESO-sponsored job to test the job board

## Need help?

PESO officers have a dedicated support channel. Contact us at **peso@localpro.ph** or use the **PESO Support** button in the portal — our team responds within one business day.
