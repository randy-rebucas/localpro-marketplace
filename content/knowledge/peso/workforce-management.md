## Overview

The workforce management tools let you enroll local workers into the LocalPro marketplace — either one at a time or in bulk — and organise them into livelihood groups for program tracking.

Access everything from **PESO Portal → Workforce** in the sidebar.

## Referring a single provider

Use individual referrals for workers who already have a mobile phone and basic digital literacy:

1. Go to **Workforce → Refer Provider**
2. Enter the worker's full name, mobile number, and primary skill/trade
3. Select a livelihood group (optional)
4. Click **Send Referral**

The worker receives an SMS with a link to create their LocalPro provider account. Your office is tagged as the referring PESO, and the worker's profile will carry your LGU's verification badge once you approve their account.

## Bulk onboarding via CSV

For large cohorts — beneficiaries from livelihood programs, TUPAD batches, or training graduates — use the CSV bulk onboard tool:

1. Go to **Workforce → Bulk Onboard**
2. Download the **CSV Template**
3. Fill in the required columns:
   - `full_name`
   - `mobile_number`
   - `primary_skill`
   - `livelihood_group` (optional)
   - `barangay`
4. Upload the completed CSV
5. Review the preview for errors
6. Click **Submit** — each valid row creates an SMS invitation

Workers with errors in their row are listed separately in a **Failed Records** report you can download, correct, and re-upload.

## Livelihood groups

Livelihood groups let you organise workers by program, batch, or trade:

- **Create a group** — Go to **Workforce → Groups → New Group** and enter a name (e.g., "TUPAD Batch 3 2026 — Carpentry")
- **Assign members** — Workers can be grouped at the point of referral or after enrolment
- **Track group outcomes** — View completion rates, active jobs, and total earnings per group in the **Reports** section

Groups are useful for reporting to DOLE, LGU councils, and livelihood program funders.

## Workforce registry

The **Registry** is a searchable directory of all providers associated with your office:

| Column | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Name | Worker's full name (links to their profile) |
| Skill | Primary service category |
| Status | Active, Pending, Suspended |
| Jobs Completed | Total jobs done through LocalPro |
| Rating | Average client rating |
| Last Active | Most recent job activity |
| Group | Livelihood group if assigned |

Filter by status, skill, group, or barangay. Export the registry to CSV for submission to LGU councils or DOLE regional offices.

## Managing worker accounts

From any worker's registry entry you can:

- **View their full provider profile** — skills, reviews, verification status
- **Issue or revoke a certification** — appears as a badge on their public profile (see Verification article)
- **Suspend from program** — removes LGU badge and flags account for review if misconduct is reported
- **Transfer to another group** — move workers between livelihood groups without losing history

## Tips

- **Verify mobile numbers before bulk upload** — an incorrectly entered number means the worker never receives their invitation.
- **Follow up with pending workers** — check the Registry filter for **Pending** status and call workers who haven't activated their accounts within 7 days.
- **Use groups for every program batch** — it makes end-of-program reporting far easier.
