## Overview

The **Reports** section gives PESO officers a dashboard of employment outcomes, livelihood program metrics, and referral activity for your office's coverage area. Use these tools to fulfil reporting obligations to DOLE, LGU councils, and program funders — and to monitor the real-world impact of your employment programs.

Access it from **PESO Portal → Reports**.

## Employment outcomes report

The main report tracks how referred workers are performing on the marketplace:

| Metric | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Workers Enrolled** | Total workers onboarded through your office (all time / period) |
| **Workers Active** | Workers who completed at least one job in the period |
| **Jobs Completed** | Total LocalPro jobs completed by your workforce |
| **Total Earnings** | Combined earnings of your enrolled workforce (before platform fee) |
| **Average Jobs per Worker** | Mean completed jobs per active worker |
| **Average Rating** | Mean client rating across your workforce |

Use the date range picker to view any period — monthly data aligns with the standard DOLE PESO Report format.

## Livelihood group reports

Drill down by livelihood group to see program-specific outcomes:

1. Go to **Reports → By Program**
2. Select a livelihood group from the dropdown
3. View the outcomes table and charts for that group
4. Export as PDF or CSV

Reports per group include a worker-level breakdown — each row shows one worker's jobs, earnings, and rating within the period. This format is suitable for submission to program sponsors and LGU councils.

## DOLE compliance report

The **DOLE Placement Report** template is pre-formatted to match the required DOLE submission fields:

- Worker name, sex, age, skill, barangay
- Employment status (self-employed via platform)
- Date of first job placement
- Number of jobs and total income earned in reporting period

To generate:

1. Go to **Reports → DOLE Placement Report**
2. Select the reporting quarter and year
3. Click **Generate**
4. Download as Excel (.xlsx) or PDF

## Referral tracking

The **Referrals** page shows the full history of workers you've referred:

- **Pending** — Invited but haven't completed account setup yet
- **Active** — Completed signup and at least one job
- **Inactive** — Enrolled but no job activity in 90+ days
- **Removed** — Workers who deactivated or were removed from your registry

For inactive workers, you can trigger a **Re-engagement SMS** directly from the list — this sends a personalised message prompting them to check new job opportunities in their area.

## Exporting data

Most report views have an **Export** button that outputs to CSV or Excel. The raw export includes full per-worker, per-job detail for custom analysis or upload into government HRMIS and LMIS systems.

## Monthly summary dashboard

The **Monthly Summary** at the top of the Reports page shows a quick snapshot:

- New workers enrolled this month
- Jobs completed this month
- Month-over-month change in earnings
- Workers who received their first job this month

Pin this screenshot to your monthly PESO activity report for your LGU executive office.

## Audit log

Every action taken by any officer in your office — referrals, verifications, certifications, job posts, broadcasts — is recorded in the **Activity Log** under **Office Settings → Audit Log**. This log is read-only and cannot be edited, providing a tamper-evident record for compliance reviews.
