## Welcome to LocalPro for Business

LocalPro Business gives companies, property managers, and commercial operators a centralised account to hire service providers at scale. Instead of individual employees managing separate client accounts, your entire organisation shares one business profile — with spending controls, member roles, and consolidated reporting.

## How it works

1. **Create your organisation** — Register your business name, address, and contact details.
2. **Choose a plan** — Select Starter, Growth, or Enterprise depending on your team size and job volume.
3. **Invite team members** — Add managers and staff who can post jobs on behalf of the business.
4. **Post jobs** — Create service jobs from the business dashboard; all bookings are tracked under your organisation.
5. **Review and approve** — Managers can review quotes and approve spending before escrow is funded.
6. **Track everything** — All jobs, invoices, and provider payments are visible in one place.

## Setting up your organisation

From your dashboard, go to **Organisation Settings** and fill in:

- **Business name and registration number**
- **Registered address** (used for distance matching with providers)
- **Primary contact** (email and phone for billing and support)
- **Branch locations** — add as many locations as needed; each can post and track its own jobs

Once submitted, your organisation is active immediately. You can refine details at any time from Organisation Settings.

## Member roles

| Role | What they can do |
|------|-----------------|
| **Owner** | Full access: billing, settings, member management, all jobs |
| **Manager** | Post jobs, review and accept quotes, approve payments, view reports |
| **Staff** | Post jobs, view status updates — cannot approve spending or view billing |

Owners can assign and change roles at any time from **Team Management**.

## Your first job

Once your organisation is set up, click **Post a Job** from the business dashboard. Jobs posted under a business account are labelled as commercial listings, which signals to providers that the work is professional and recurring potential is high — often attracting more experienced quotes.

## Tips for getting started

- **Add all branch locations upfront** so jobs are automatically matched to the nearest providers.
- **Set spending limits per manager** to keep costs predictable across large teams.
- **Use job templates** (available on Growth and Enterprise) to speed up recurring job creation.
- **Request invoices** from your Billing page for accounting and tax purposes.
