## Business plans

LocalPro offers three plans for business accounts. All plans include the core marketplace features; higher tiers unlock volume tools and dedicated support.

| Feature | Starter | Growth | Enterprise |
|---------|---------|--------|------------|
| Team members | Up to 3 | Up to 15 | Unlimited |
| Branch locations | 1 | Up to 5 | Unlimited |
| Job templates | ❌ | ✅ | ✅ |
| Spend approval workflow | ❌ | ✅ | ✅ |
| Advanced analytics | ❌ | ✅ | ✅ |
| Priority support | ❌ | ❌ | ✅ |
| Dedicated account manager | ❌ | ❌ | ✅ |
| Custom platform fee rate | ❌ | ❌ | Negotiated |
| Monthly subscription | Free | ₱1,500/mo | Contact us |

> The platform fee (applied to each completed job) is separate from the subscription fee.

## Upgrading or downgrading

To change your plan:

1. Go to **Organisation Settings → Billing**
2. Click **Change Plan**
3. Select your new plan and confirm

Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades take effect at the end of the current billing cycle — you retain access to higher-tier features until then.

## Invoices and receipts

All subscription charges and job-related fees are itemised on your monthly invoice:

- Go to **Organisation Settings → Billing → Invoices**
- Download PDF invoices for any billing period
- Invoices include your business registration name and TIN if provided

For VAT-registered businesses, ensure your TIN is entered in Organisation Settings so it appears on all invoices.

## Payment methods

Business subscriptions are charged to a saved card. To update your payment method:

1. Go to **Billing → Payment Methods**
2. Click **Add Card** or **Replace Card**
3. Enter your card details (Visa, Mastercard, or JCB)

Subscription auto-renews each month. You'll receive a reminder email 7 days before renewal. If payment fails, your account enters a 3-day grace period before being downgraded to Starter.

## Job transaction fees

In addition to the subscription, LocalPro charges a **platform fee** on each completed job. The fee is deducted from the escrow payout to the provider — it does not appear as a separate charge to your business.

On Enterprise plans, custom rates below the standard 15% are available — contact your account manager.

## Cancelling your subscription

To cancel:

1. Go to **Billing → Manage Subscription**
2. Click **Cancel Subscription**
3. Confirm the cancellation

Your account continues on the Growth/Enterprise plan until the end of the paid period, then reverts to Starter. Your data, jobs, and team members are retained.

## Refunds

Subscription fees are non-refundable. For job-related payment disputes, refer to the [Disputes and Refunds](#) article.
