## Posting a service job

From the business dashboard, click **Post a Job**. The process is similar to a standard client job with additional business-specific options.

### Required fields

- **Category** — Choose the service type (Plumbing, Electrical, Cleaning, etc.)
- **Title and description** — Be specific about the scope, location, and any special requirements
- **Budget** — Enter the amount you're willing to pay; providers quote against this figure
- **Site location** — Select the branch or custom address where the work will be done

### Business-only options

- **Assign to branch** — Route the job to a specific branch for location-based reporting
- **Assign to manager** — Nominate a team member to handle quote review and approval
- **Recurring schedule** — Set a repeat frequency (weekly, monthly, quarterly) for maintenance jobs
- **Internal reference** — Add a PO number or internal job code for your accounting system
- **Spending limit override** — Managers with permission can raise the per-job limit for large projects

## Using job templates

On **Growth** and **Enterprise** plans, you can save a job as a template:

1. Complete a job form and click **Save as Template** before submitting
2. Name the template (e.g., "Monthly HVAC Maintenance")
3. Next time, go to **Templates** and click **Use Template** to pre-fill the form

Templates save time for recurring jobs and ensure consistency across branches.

## Approval workflow

If your organisation has enabled **spend approvals**, jobs posted by staff require manager sign-off before going live:

1. Staff member submits the job — it enters **Pending Approval** status
2. The assigned manager receives a notification
3. Manager reviews and clicks **Approve** — the job is published to the marketplace
4. If rejected, staff receives a note and can revise before resubmitting

Spend approval settings are configured per role in **Organisation Settings → Approvals**.

## Managing posted jobs

- **All Jobs** in the sidebar shows every job across all branches with filter options by status, branch, and manager
- Click any job to view quotes, messages, and the current status stage
- Managers can accept quotes and trigger escrow funding on behalf of the organisation
- Jobs can be duplicated from the job menu for quick re-posting of similar work

## Closing or cancelling a job

To close a job before a quote is accepted, go to the job page and click **Close Job**. If a provider is already assigned, cancellation may incur a fee depending on the job status — see the [Disputes and Refunds](#) article for details.

## Best practices

- **Write detailed descriptions** especially for commercial or specialised jobs. Providers give more accurate quotes with clear scope.
- **Use internal references** to reconcile LocalPro invoices with your accounts payable system.
- **Flag recurring jobs** so providers know they may be invited back, which improves quote quality.
