## Your team on LocalPro

Business accounts support multiple team members who can create and manage jobs on behalf of your organisation. Each member is assigned a role that controls what they can see and do.

## Inviting team members

1. Go to **Organisation Settings → Team Members**
2. Click **Invite Member**
3. Enter the team member's email address
4. Select their role: **Manager** or **Staff**
5. Click **Send Invite**

The invitee receives an email with a link to create their LocalPro account (or log into their existing account) and join your organisation. Invites expire after 7 days — you can resend from the Team Members page.

## Roles explained

### Owner
- Full control over the organisation account
- Manages billing, subscription, and integrations
- Can add, remove, or change the roles of all members
- Views all jobs, reports, and financials across all branches
- Only the owner can transfer ownership or delete the organisation

### Manager
- Creates and manages jobs on behalf of the organisation
- Reviews and accepts provider quotes
- Approves or rejects staff job submissions (if spend approval is enabled)
- Views reports for their assigned branches
- Cannot access billing or change other members' roles

### Staff
- Creates job submissions (sent for manager approval if approvals are enabled)
- Views job status and communicates with providers on assigned jobs
- Cannot approve spending, view billing, or access organisation settings

## Spending limits

You can set a per-job spending limit for managers to prevent unauthorised large purchases:

1. Go to **Organisation Settings → Spending Controls**
2. Set the **Manager Spending Limit** (e.g., ₱25,000 per job)
3. Save settings

Jobs above this limit require Owner approval before going live. Enterprise accounts can set limits per individual manager.

## Assigning members to branches

If your organisation has multiple branches, you can restrict members to a specific branch:

1. Open the member's profile from the Team Members page
2. Select **Branch Access** and choose one or more branches
3. Save — the member will only see jobs and reports for their assigned branches

## Removing a team member

To remove someone from your organisation:

1. Go to **Organisation Settings → Team Members**
2. Find the member and click **Remove**
3. Confirm the action

Removed members lose access immediately. Any jobs they created remain under the organisation and can be managed by other members.

## Activity log

Every team member action — job creation, quote acceptance, approval, payout request — is recorded in the **Activity Log** under Organisation Settings. This provides a full audit trail for compliance and internal reviews.
