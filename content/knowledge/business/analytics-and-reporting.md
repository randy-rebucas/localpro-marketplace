## Overview

The **Analytics** section (Growth and Enterprise plans) gives you a complete picture of your organisation's service activity — spending trends, provider performance, job completion rates, and branch comparisons — all in one place.

Access it from **Dashboard → Analytics** in the sidebar.

## Spending summary

The top of the Analytics page shows a snapshot for the selected date range:

- **Total spend** — Total escrow released across all jobs
- **Active jobs** — Jobs currently in progress
- **Completed jobs** — Jobs marked as done in the period
- **Average job value** — Mean escrow amount across completed jobs
- **Platform fees paid** — Fee totals for the period

Use the date range picker (7 days, 30 days, 3 months, custom) to adjust the view.

## Spending by branch

The **Branch Breakdown** chart shows spending per branch location for the selected period. This helps property managers and multi-site operators identify which locations are most active and where costs are concentrated.

Click any branch bar to drill down into that branch's individual jobs.

## Top categories

See which service categories (Plumbing, Electrical, Cleaning, etc.) account for the most spend. Useful for budget planning and vendor contract negotiations.

## Provider performance

The **Provider Leaderboard** lists the providers your organisation has hired most often, along with:

- Number of jobs completed
- Average star rating
- Total spend with that provider
- On-time completion rate

Use this to identify reliable providers worth re-engaging for future work, or to spot patterns of underperformance.

## Job completion rate

Tracks the percentage of posted jobs that result in a completed, paid outcome vs. jobs closed without hire, disputed, or abandoned. A low completion rate may indicate budget mismatches or unclear job descriptions.

## Team activity

On Enterprise plans, the **Team Activity** report shows a per-member breakdown:

- Jobs posted per member
- Total spend approved
- Average quote review time
- Approval rejection rate

Useful for manager performance reviews and identifying bottlenecks in the approval workflow.

## Exporting data

To export report data:

1. Go to **Analytics**
2. Apply the desired filters and date range
3. Click **Export** and choose **CSV** or **PDF**

The CSV export includes one row per job with columns for job ID, title, category, branch, assigned manager, provider, budget, final amount, escrow release date, and platform fee. Use this to reconcile with your accounting system or generate cost centre reports.

## Scheduled reports

Enterprise accounts can schedule automated reports to be emailed to selected team members on a weekly or monthly basis:

1. Go to **Analytics → Scheduled Reports**
2. Click **New Scheduled Report**
3. Choose the report type, frequency, recipients, and format
4. Save — the first report sends at the next scheduled interval
