## Why your profile matters

Your agency profile is your storefront on LocalPro. When a client browses quotes, they compare provider and agency profiles side by side. A strong profile — with a professional logo, detailed bio, clear service list, and positive reviews — directly improves your win rate.

## Profile elements

### Agency name and logo

Your agency name is your brand. Use the same name as your registered business. Upload a square logo (minimum 200×200 px, PNG or JPG) that looks clean at small sizes — it appears next to every quote you submit.

To update: **Agency Settings → Profile → Logo**

### Bio / About section

Write 2–4 sentences that tell clients:

- What your agency specialises in
- How many years you've been operating
- What makes you different (certifications, speed, warranty, etc.)
- The areas you serve

**Example:**  
*"FastFix Services is a licensed electrical and plumbing contractor with 8 years of experience serving Metro Manila. Our 12-person team is fully insured and background-checked. We guarantee on-time arrival and offer a 30-day workmanship warranty on all jobs."*

### Service categories

List every category your team can handle. Clients filter the marketplace by category, so being listed in the right ones ensures you appear in relevant searches.

Add or remove categories from **Agency Settings → Services**.

### Service areas

Select the cities, municipalities, or regions you cover. Be accurate — accepting jobs outside your actual service area leads to cancellations and damages your rating.

### Response time and availability

Keep your availability flag updated. Agencies marked as available appear higher in quote comparisons. If your team is fully booked, set yourself to **Unavailable** to pause incoming job requests temporarily.

## Certifications and compliance badges

Verified credentials build trust with clients. Upload copies of:

- **Business registration** (DTI or SEC certificate) — Required for agency approval
- **Trade licenses** — Electrical, plumbing, or other relevant PRC/TESDA certifications
- **Insurance documents** — Liability insurance certificate if applicable

Approved documents appear as badges on your public profile. Certification review by our team typically takes 2–3 business days.

To upload: **Agency Settings → Credentials**

## Ratings and reviews

Your agency rating is the average star rating from clients whose jobs you've completed. It appears prominently on your profile and in search results.

- Ratings are left by clients after a job is confirmed complete
- Each rating reflects both the agency's coordination and the assigned staff member's work quality
- You cannot remove or dispute a review unless it violates LocalPro's content policy

### Improving your rating

- **Communicate proactively** — clients rate responsiveness highly
- **Assign the right person** — match staff skills to job requirements
- **Follow up after completion** — a quick message asking if everything is satisfactory goes a long way
- **Resolve issues before they become disputes** — proactive problem-solving preserves ratings
