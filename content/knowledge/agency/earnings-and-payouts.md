## How agency earnings work

When a job is completed and the client confirms, escrow is released. The payment goes to your **agency wallet** — not directly to the assigned staff member. From there, you manage how earnings are distributed within your team.

## Platform fee

LocalPro charges a **15% platform fee** on each completed job, deducted at the time of escrow release.

**Example:**
- Client paid ₱8,000 into escrow
- Platform fee: ₱1,200 (15%)
- **Agency receives: ₱6,800**

The platform fee covers payment processing, dispute mediation, platform maintenance, and customer support. Enterprise agencies may negotiate a reduced rate with their account manager.

## Agency wallet

Your agency wallet balance is shown on the **Agency Dashboard → Earnings** page. It accumulates as jobs are completed and is drawn down when you:

- Withdraw funds to your bank account or e-wallet
- Pay out staff members for completed job shares

## Staff payouts

As an agency owner, you decide how much each staff member receives per job. There is no fixed split — you set the payout amount when disbursing funds. To pay a staff member:

1. Go to **Earnings → Staff Payouts**
2. Click **New Payout**
3. Select the staff member
4. Enter the payout amount
5. Add an optional reference note (e.g., job ID)
6. Confirm — funds are deducted from the agency wallet and credited to the staff member's LocalPro account

Staff members can then withdraw their balance to their own bank account or e-wallet. They manage their own tax obligations independently.

## Withdrawing agency funds

To withdraw your agency balance:

1. Go to **Earnings → Withdraw**
2. Enter the amount (minimum ₱500)
3. Select your linked bank account or e-wallet
4. Confirm

Withdrawals are processed within **1–3 business days**. You'll receive a notification when the transfer is completed.

## Adding a bank account or e-wallet

Before your first withdrawal, add a payment method:

1. Go to **Earnings → Payment Methods**
2. Click **Add Account**
3. Enter bank account or e-wallet details
4. Verify via SMS confirmation

Supported: BPI, BDO, UnionBank, GCash, Maya.

## Transaction history

The full earnings history is available on the **Earnings** page:

- Per-job breakdown with job ID, client, and amount
- Platform fees deducted per job
- Staff payouts made (date, member, amount)
- Withdrawal history with transfer status

Export to CSV from the **Transactions** tab for payroll records or tax reporting.

## Important notes

- **Staff members are not employees of LocalPro** — they are workers managed by your agency. Employment classification and tax compliance are your responsibility.
- **Withholding tax** — If your agency is subject to expanded withholding tax (EWT), consult a tax professional about your obligations on platform income.
- Commission and revenue-sharing arrangements between the agency and staff are managed privately — LocalPro does not enforce or audit internal pay splits.
