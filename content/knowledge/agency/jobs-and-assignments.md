## How agency jobs work

When your agency wins a job, the work doesn't go to a single person — it goes to a slot in your agency's job queue. You then assign it to the most appropriate available staff member. This gives you flexibility to manage workload, match skills to tasks, and maintain consistent quality under your brand.

## Browsing and quoting jobs

Your agency appears in the marketplace and receives job opportunities the same way individual providers do. To submit a quote:

1. Go to **Jobs → Browse** in the agency dashboard
2. Filter by category, location, and budget
3. Click a job and review the details
4. Click **Submit Quote** — enter your price and a message
5. Submit — the client will see your agency name and profile

Clients choose between your agency and individual providers on the same basis: price, profile quality, and ratings.

## Receiving a job assignment

When a client accepts your agency's quote:

- The job status changes to **Assigned**
- You receive a notification to assign the job to a staff member
- A 24-hour assignment window applies — unassigned jobs can be flagged by clients

To assign:
1. Go to **Jobs → Active**
2. Open the newly assigned job
3. Click **Assign Staff Member**
4. Choose an available worker from your roster
5. Confirm — the worker receives a notification with job details

## Reassigning a job

Sometimes you need to move a job from one staff member to another:

1. Open the job from **Jobs → Active**
2. Click **Reassign**
3. Select a different available staff member
4. Clients are notified of the change automatically

You can reassign at any point while the job is in progress, as long as the job has not yet been marked complete.

## Job status stages

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **Open** | Posted by client; accepting quotes |
| **Assigned** | Your quote was accepted; awaiting staff assignment |
| **In Progress** | Staff member is actively working on the job |
| **Pending Completion** | Staff marked work done; awaiting client confirmation |
| **Completed** | Client confirmed; escrow released to agency |
| **Disputed** | Client raised a dispute; under review |

## Milestones

For large or multi-phase jobs, your agency can propose milestones — partial payments tied to specific deliverables. Request milestone splits from the job page before work begins. Each milestone is funded and released separately.

## Job communication

All client communication happens through the in-app message thread on the job. Both the agency owner and the assigned staff member can send and receive messages. Keep communication inside the app:

- It protects both parties in case of a dispute
- It provides a record that our support team can review
- It keeps your agency's brand consistent

## If a job is cancelled

If a client cancels after your agency is assigned, the cancellation policy applies based on the job stage. If work has already started, you may be entitled to partial payment. For early-stage cancellations, see the [Disputes and Refunds](#) knowledge article.
