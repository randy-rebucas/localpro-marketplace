## Welcome to LocalPro for Agencies

LocalPro Agency accounts let you operate as a team of service providers under a single, branded profile. Instead of individual providers competing separately, your agency wins jobs and assigns them to the right staff member — with centralised earnings, reporting, and client communication.

Agencies are ideal for home services companies, cleaning firms operational teams, and maintenance contractors who employ or subcontract multiple workers.

## How it works

1. **Register your agency** — Create an agency profile with your business name, logo, and service categories.
2. **Get approved** — Our team reviews agency accounts within 2–3 business days.
3. **Invite staff** — Add your workers as staff members linked to your agency.
4. **Bid on jobs** — Your agency submits quotes; winning jobs are assigned to available staff.
5. **Staff complete the work** — Workers receive job details, communicate with clients, and upload completion photos.
6. **Get paid** — Earnings flow to the agency account; you manage staff payouts from there.

## Setting up your agency profile

After signing up as an agency, complete your profile before your application is reviewed:

- **Agency name and logo** — This is your public brand on LocalPro
- **Business registration details** — Required for account approval
- **Service categories** — The trades or services your team provides
- **Service areas** — The cities or regions you cover
- **Bio / About section** — Tell clients what makes your agency different

A complete, professional profile significantly improves your approval chances and win rates.

## Agency vs. individual provider

| Feature | Individual Provider | Agency |
|---------|--------------------|----|
| Jobs assigned to | Single provider | Any staff member |
| Earnings management | Personal wallet | Agency wallet + staff payouts |
| Client-facing brand | Personal profile | Agency logo and name |
| Staff capacity | Single person | Multiple workers |
| Quote volume | Limited by one person | Scale with team size |

## After approval

Once your agency is approved:

- Your agency profile appears in the marketplace alongside individual providers
- You can start receiving and submitting quotes
- Staff members can be invited and begin receiving job assignments
- Your agency rating builds as jobs are completed

## Tips for new agencies

- **Complete your profile fully** before submitting for approval — incomplete profiles are often delayed.
- **Start with 2–3 core categories** rather than listing every possible service; focused agencies win more jobs.
- **Assign jobs quickly** — clients notice fast response times and it improves your agency's completion rate.
