## Why your profile matters

Your profile is your storefront on LocalPro. Clients browse provider profiles before accepting quotes. A complete, professional profile with strong reviews can be the difference between winning and losing a job.

## Profile sections

### Bio
Write a 2–4 sentence bio that describes:
- Your trade and years of experience
- Any specialisations or certifications
- Your approach to client service

**Example:** *"Licensed electrician with 8 years of experience in residential and light commercial work. Specialising in rewiring, panel upgrades, and smart home installations. I pride myself on clean, code-compliant work and clear communication throughout every project."*

You can use the **AI Bio Generator** on your profile page to get a starting point based on your skills.

### Skills
Add specific skills that clients search for (e.g., "Leak repair", "Tile installation", "Air conditioning service"). Skills appear as tags on your profile and improve your visibility in search results.

### Profile photo
Upload a clear, professional headshot or your business logo. Profiles with photos receive significantly more quote responses than those without.

### Service areas
Add the areas where you're willing to work. Clients in your service area see you first in search results. You can add multiple areas and set a coverage radius for each.

### Availability schedule
Set your working days and hours. This helps clients understand when you're available to start work and reduces scheduling mismatches.

### Portfolio / service photos
Upload photos of past work to showcase your quality. Before-and-after photos are especially effective.

## Profile completeness score

Your profile page shows a completeness percentage. Aim for 100% — fully complete profiles are ranked higher in the marketplace and receive more client views.

## Keeping your profile updated

- Update your availability when you're fully booked to avoid accepting jobs you can't handle
- Add new skills as you expand your service offering
- Keep your bio current if you earn new certifications or change your focus
