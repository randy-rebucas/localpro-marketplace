## What is KYC?

KYC stands for **Know Your Customer** — a standard identity verification process used by financial platforms. LocalPro requires KYC verification to ensure providers are who they say they are, which builds trust with clients and keeps the marketplace safe.

## Why do I need to complete KYC?

KYC verification is required to:
- Receive payouts from completed jobs
- Build verified trust with clients (a "Verified" badge appears on your profile)
- Access certain platform features reserved for verified providers

## What documents are required?

You'll need to submit at least one of the following government-issued IDs:

- **Valid passport**
- **National ID (PhilSys)**
- **Driver's licence**
- **SSS, GSIS, or Postal ID**
- **PRC licence** (if applicable to your trade)

For business accounts, you may also be asked for your **DTI/SEC registration** and **BIR certificate of registration**.

## How to submit your KYC documents

1. Go to your **Profile** page
2. Scroll to the **Identity Verification** section
3. Click **Submit Documents**
4. Upload clear, readable photos or scans of your documents
5. Click **Submit for Review**

Make sure:
- All four corners of the document are visible
- The image is in focus and not glare-affected
- The name on the document matches your LocalPro profile name

## What happens after I submit?

Our team reviews KYC submissions within **1–3 business days**. You'll receive an email with the outcome:

- **Approved** — A verified badge is added to your profile
- **Rejected** — You'll receive feedback on what needs to be corrected and can resubmit

## Common rejection reasons

- Photo too blurry or partially cropped
- Document is expired
- Name mismatch between document and profile
- Wrong document type submitted

## Is my information secure?

Yes. KYC documents are stored with encryption and are only accessed by authorised staff for verification purposes. We never share your documents with clients or third parties.
