## Welcome to LocalPro

LocalPro is a marketplace that connects skilled service providers with local clients who need your expertise. As a provider, you set your own prices, choose the jobs you want, and get paid securely through our escrow system.

## How it works

1. **Complete your profile** — Add your bio, skills, service photos, and availability schedule.
2. **Get approved** — Our team reviews your account (usually within 1–2 business days).
3. **Browse the marketplace** — View open jobs in your area and categories.
4. **Submit quotes** — Send a price and message to clients whose jobs match your skills.
5. **Win the job** — If the client accepts your quote, the job is assigned to you.
6. **Do the work** — Communicate through the app, complete the job, and upload completion photos.
7. **Get paid** — Once the client confirms completion, funds are released to your account.

## Your earnings

LocalPro charges a **15% platform fee** on each completed job. For example, if you quote ₱5,000, you receive ₱4,250 after the platform fee. This fee covers payment processing, platform maintenance, customer support, and dispute mediation.

## Account approval

New provider accounts go through a one-time approval process. You can set up your profile while waiting, but you won't appear in the marketplace until your account is approved. You'll receive an email notification once approved.

To speed up approval:
- Complete your profile fully (bio, skills, at least one profile photo)
- Submit your KYC documents
- Add your service areas

## Getting your first job

First-time providers often find success by:
- Setting competitive prices for their first 2–3 jobs to build reviews
- Writing personalised, detailed quotes (not just a price)
- Responding to client messages within a few hours
- Keeping their profile and availability up to date

Once you have 5+ positive reviews, your profile becomes significantly more attractive to new clients.
