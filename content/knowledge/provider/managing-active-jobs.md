## Job lifecycle

Once a client accepts your quote, the job moves through the following stages:

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **Assigned** | Client accepted your quote. Waiting for escrow to be funded. |
| **In Progress** | Escrow funded. Work can begin. |
| **Completed** | You have marked the job complete. Awaiting client confirmation. |
| **Released** | Client confirmed. Funds released to your account. |

## Step 1 — Wait for escrow funding

After a quote is accepted, you'll receive a notification when the client funds the escrow. **Do not begin work until escrow is funded** — this protects you from non-payment.

If the client hasn't funded escrow within 48 hours, you can send them a reminder via in-app messaging.

## Step 2 — Start the job

Once escrow is funded:
1. Go to **Active Jobs** in the sidebar
2. Open the job
3. Click **Start Job** to change the status to In Progress
4. This timestamp is recorded and visible to both parties

## Step 3 — Communicate throughout

Use the **Messages** tab on the job to:
- Confirm arrival time with the client
- Update them on progress
- Flag any unexpected issues (hidden damage, scope changes)
- Share progress photos

Good communication is the #1 factor in positive reviews.

## Step 4 — Handle scope changes

If the actual work turns out to be more than the original quote, **discuss and agree on a new price before proceeding**. Do not assume the client will pay more without prior agreement. Document scope changes in writing via messages.

## Step 5 — Mark the job complete

When the work is done:
1. Upload at least one **completion photo** (required)
2. Click **Mark as Complete**
3. The client receives a notification to review and confirm

The client has 7 days to confirm or raise a dispute. If no action is taken, funds are automatically released.

## Step 6 — Get paid

Once funds are released, they appear in your **Earnings** balance. You can request a payout from the **Payouts** page at any time (subject to minimum payout amount).

## What if the client disputes the job?

If a client opens a dispute, do not panic:
- Respond professionally to the support team's questions
- Provide any photos, messages, or evidence of completed work
- Our team will mediate and make a fair decision
- Most disputes are resolved within 3–5 business days
