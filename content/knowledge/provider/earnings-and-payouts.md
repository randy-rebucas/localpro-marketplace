## How earnings work

Every time a job is completed and escrow is released, your earnings are credited to your LocalPro balance. You can track your balance and payment history on the **Earnings** page.

## Platform fee

LocalPro charges a **20% platform fee** on each completed job. This is automatically deducted when funds are released.

**Example:**
- You quote ₱5,000 for a job
- Client pays ₱5,000 into escrow
- Job is completed and confirmed
- Platform fee: ₱1,000 (20%)
- **You receive: ₱4,000**

The fee covers payment processing, customer support, dispute mediation, and platform maintenance.

## Provider tiers and reduced fees

As you complete more jobs and maintain a high rating, you move through provider tiers:

| Tier | Completed Jobs | Platform Fee |
|------|---------------|-------------|
| Starter | 0–4 | 20% |
| Rising | 5–19 | 9% |
| Pro | 20–49 | 8% |
| Elite | 50+ | 7% |

Your current tier and fee rate are shown on your **Earnings** page and your profile.

## Requesting a payout

1. Go to **Payouts** in the sidebar
2. Click **Request Payout**
3. Enter the amount you'd like to withdraw (minimum ₱500)
4. Select your bank account or e-wallet
5. Confirm the request

Payouts are processed within **1–3 business days**. You'll receive a notification when the transfer is sent.

## Adding a bank account or e-wallet

Before requesting your first payout, add your payment details:
1. Go to **Payouts**
2. Click **Add Payment Method**
3. Enter your bank account number or e-wallet details
4. Verify with a confirmation code (sent via SMS)

Supported methods: BPI, BDO, UnionBank, GCash, Maya.

## Earnings history

Your full earnings history is available on the **Earnings** page, including:
- Per-job breakdown
- Platform fees deducted
- Payout dates and amounts
- Pending balance vs. available balance

## Tax and compliance

LocalPro does not withhold income tax on your behalf. Providers are responsible for declaring their earnings and paying the appropriate taxes under Philippine law. You can export your transaction history as a CSV from the **Transactions** page for accounting purposes.
