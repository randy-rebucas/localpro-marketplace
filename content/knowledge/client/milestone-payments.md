## What are milestone payments?

Milestone payments let you fund a single escrow and release it in stages — one portion after each phase of work is completed. This is ideal for larger or multi-day projects like renovations, construction, or any job broken into distinct phases.

Instead of releasing the full amount at the end, you release funds as you verify each milestone, giving you control throughout the project.

## When to use milestones

Milestones work best when:

- The job spans multiple days or visits
- Work is broken into clear phases (e.g., demo → framing → finishing)
- The total budget is large and releasing it all at once feels risky
- You agreed on a phased payment schedule with your provider before the job started

## How to release a partial payment

1. Go to **My Jobs** and open the job
2. Click **Release Partial Payment**
3. Enter the amount to release for the completed phase
4. Confirm — funds are sent to the provider immediately

The remaining balance stays locked in escrow until you release it or the job is fully completed.

## Milestone payment example

| Phase | Amount | Released when |
|-------|--------|---------------|
| Demolition & prep | ₱3,000 | After site is cleared |
| Framing & rough work | ₱5,000 | After structural work done |
| Final finishing | ₱4,000 | After full completion confirmed |
| **Total** | **₱12,000** | |

You fund the full ₱12,000 upfront into escrow, then release each portion as you approve that phase.

## Auto dispute trigger

If a provider marks a milestone or the full job as complete but you believe the work is not done:

- **Do not release funds** — take time to review
- If you take no action within **7 days** of the provider marking complete, funds release automatically
- To pause this clock, open a **Dispute** from the job page — escrow is frozen until resolved

This auto-release protects providers from indefinite payment holds while the 7-day window gives you time to properly review the work.

## Digital completion confirmation

When the provider marks work complete, you will receive a notification to review and confirm. To confirm:

1. Open the job page
2. Review any after-photos uploaded by the provider
3. Click **Confirm Completion** if you're satisfied, or **Open Dispute** if not

Your digital confirmation acts as a signed acknowledgment that the work was completed as agreed.

## Tips for smooth milestone projects

- **Agree on milestones before work starts** — discuss phases and amounts with the provider in the job chat
- **Document each phase** — request the provider to upload photos before you release each partial payment
- **Don't skip review** — even if you trust the provider, take a moment to verify each phase before releasing funds
- **Partial releases are final** — once you release a portion, it cannot be recalled; only open escrow can be disputed

## What if the project runs late?

If a provider misses an agreed timeline:

1. Message them first through the job chat to understand the delay
2. If they are unresponsive or the delay is unreasonable, open a **Dispute**
3. Our support team will assess whether a partial refund or extension is appropriate

Funds remain frozen in escrow during any active dispute.
