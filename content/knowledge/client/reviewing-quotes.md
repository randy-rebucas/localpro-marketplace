## Receiving quotes

Once your job is live, providers can submit quotes. You'll receive an in-app notification and email for each new quote. All quotes for your job appear on the job detail page under the **Quotes** tab.

## What a quote contains

Each quote includes:
- **Quoted price** — the provider's fee for completing the job
- **Message** — a personalised note from the provider explaining their approach
- **Provider profile** — rating, completed jobs, review count, skills, and bio

## Comparing providers

Before accepting, take time to review each provider's profile:

- **Star rating** — based on verified client reviews
- **Completed jobs** — a measure of experience on the platform
- **Response rate** — how quickly they typically respond to messages
- **Bio and skills** — their stated expertise and qualifications
- **Reviews** — read what previous clients have said about quality and professionalism

## Messaging a provider before accepting

You can send a message to any provider who quoted on your job before making a decision. Use this to:
- Ask clarifying questions about their approach
- Confirm availability and timeline
- Negotiate the price if appropriate

Click **Message** on any quote card to start a conversation.

## Accepting a quote

When you're ready to hire, click **Accept Quote** on the provider you want. This:
- Notifies the provider they've been selected
- Changes the job status to **Assigned**
- Prompts you to fund the escrow before work begins

All other providers are notified that the job has been filled.

## Can I change my mind after accepting?

If the escrow has not yet been funded and work hasn't started, contact support and we can assist. Once the job is **In Progress** (escrow funded and work started), cancellation requires a dispute process.
