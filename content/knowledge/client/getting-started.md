## Welcome to LocalPro

LocalPro is a marketplace that connects you with verified local service providers. Whether you need a plumber, electrician, cleaner, or any other home service professional, LocalPro makes it easy to post a job, receive quotes, and get the work done — safely and securely.

## How it works

1. **Post a job** — Describe the service you need, set your budget, and publish your listing.
2. **Receive quotes** — Verified providers in your area submit quotes with their price and timeline.
3. **Choose a provider** — Review profiles, ratings, and past reviews to pick the best match.
4. **Fund escrow** — Pay into a secure escrow account. Your money is held safely until the job is done.
5. **Job completed** — Once you confirm the work is finished, the funds are released to the provider.
6. **Leave a review** — Share your experience to help other clients and reward great providers.

## What you can do as a client

- Post unlimited service jobs in any category
- Browse and compare provider profiles before accepting a quote
- Communicate directly with providers via in-app messaging
- Track job progress through each status stage
- Request partial fund releases for milestone-based work
- Open a dispute if an issue arises and our support team will mediate

## Tips for a smooth experience

- **Be specific** when describing your job. Include dimensions, materials, or any special requirements.
- **Set a realistic budget** to attract quality providers. Unusually low budgets may deter skilled professionals.
- **Respond promptly** to provider messages — faster communication leads to faster job completion.
- **Fund escrow early** once you accept a quote so the provider can begin work without delay.
