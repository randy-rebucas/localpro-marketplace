## What is the Rewards Program?

Every time you complete a job through LocalPro, you earn **loyalty points**. Points accumulate toward higher membership tiers and can be converted into **cashback credits** that reduce the cost of future jobs.

## Earning Points

| Action | Points Earned |
|---|---|
| Job completed (escrow released) | 1 pt per ₱10 spent |
| First job ever | +100 bonus pts |
| Leaving a review | +50 pts |
| Friend completes their first job (referral) | +200 pts |
| Your first job as a referred member | +100 bonus pts |

**Example:** You pay ₱5,000 for a cleaning job → you earn **500 pts** (plus 100 bonus if it's your first job = 600 pts total).

> Gold and Platinum members earn a multiplier on top of base points — see the Tiers section below.

## Member Tiers

Your tier is based on your **lifetime points** (total points ever earned, including redeemed ones). Tiers are permanent and never drop.

| Tier | Lifetime Points | Bonus |
|---|---|---|
| ⭐ Standard | 0 – 499 | — |
| 🥈 Silver | 500 – 1,999 | Priority support |
| 🥇 Gold | 2,000 – 4,999 | 5% bonus points per job |
| 💎 Platinum | 5,000 + | 10% bonus points per job |

**Gold example:** A ₱5,000 job earns 500 base pts × 1.05 = **525 pts**.

Your current tier and progress to the next one are shown on your **Rewards** page and your dashboard.

## Cashback Credits

Redeem your points for **₱ cashback credits** that apply automatically to your next job payment.

- **100 pts = ₱10 credit**
- Minimum redemption: **500 pts** (= ₱50)
- Points must be redeemed in multiples of 100

**How to redeem:**
1. Go to **Rewards** in the sidebar
2. Click **Redeem Points**
3. Enter how many points you want to convert (minimum 500)
4. Confirm — your credits balance is updated instantly

Credits are shown at the payment screen and deducted automatically before processing the remainder via your payment method.

## Checking Your Balance

Visit **Rewards** in the sidebar to see:
- Your current tier and progress bar to the next tier
- Redeemable points balance
- Cashback credits available
- Recent points activity (what you earned and when)

A summary widget also appears on your **Dashboard** at a glance.
