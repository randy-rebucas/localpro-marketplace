## Why reviews matter

Reviews are the backbone of trust on LocalPro. They help other clients find quality providers and give excellent providers the recognition they deserve. Providers with consistently high ratings are more likely to appear at the top of search results and gain more work.

## When can I leave a review?

You can leave a review once a job reaches **Completed** status. You'll receive a prompt in the app and via email reminding you to share your experience.

## How to leave a review

1. Go to **My Jobs** in the sidebar
2. Open the completed job
3. Click **Leave a Review**
4. Rate the provider from 1 to 5 stars
5. Write a comment describing your experience
6. Click **Submit**

## What to include in your review

A helpful review covers:
- **Quality of work** — Was the job done to a high standard?
- **Professionalism** — Was the provider polite, punctual, and communicative?
- **Accuracy** — Did the work match the quoted price and timeline?
- **Would you hire again?** — A simple sentence like "Would definitely use again" carries a lot of weight.

## Tips for a fair review

- Be specific rather than vague. "Fixed the leak perfectly, cleaned up afterwards, and finished in under 2 hours" is more useful than "Good job."
- Separate the job outcome from pricing. If you negotiated a very low price and got average results, that may be a fair trade.
- Review the provider, not circumstances outside their control (e.g., supply delays, weather).

## Can I edit or delete a review?

Reviews can be edited within 48 hours of submission. After that they are locked to maintain integrity. If you believe a review was submitted in error, contact support.

## Can providers respond to reviews?

Yes — providers can post a professional response to any review. Responses are visible to everyone.
