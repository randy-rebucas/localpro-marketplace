## Your client profile

Your profile is visible to providers when they view job listings. A complete profile helps build trust and may attract better quotes.

To update your profile, go to **My Profile** in the sidebar.

### What you can update

- **Name** — Your display name shown to providers
- **Profile photo** — Upload a clear headshot or logo
- **Phone number** — Used for verification and urgent contact
- **Email address** — Used for notifications and login

## Managing addresses

Saved addresses make job posting faster. You can save multiple addresses (home, office, rental property) and set a default.

To manage addresses:
1. Go to **My Profile**
2. Scroll to the **Addresses** section
3. Click **Add Address** to save a new location
4. Click the **star icon** to set any address as default

When posting a job, your default address is pre-filled automatically.

## Changing your password

1. Go to **My Profile**
2. Scroll to **Security**
3. Click **Change Password**
4. Enter your current password and your new password (minimum 8 characters)
5. Click **Save**

If you've forgotten your password, use the **Forgot Password** link on the login page to reset it via email.

## Email notifications

You receive email notifications for:
- New quotes on your jobs
- Quote accepted confirmation
- Provider messages
- Escrow reminders
- Job status updates

To manage notification preferences, visit **Notifications** in the sidebar.

## Deleting your account

To delete your account, contact our support team. Please note that active jobs with funded escrow must be resolved before an account can be deleted.

## Account security tips

- Use a strong, unique password for your LocalPro account
- Do not share your login credentials with anyone
- Log out from shared or public devices
- Report any suspicious activity to support immediately
