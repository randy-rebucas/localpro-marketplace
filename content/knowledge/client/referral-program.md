## How Referrals Work

LocalPro rewards you for growing the community. When you invite a friend who goes on to complete their first job, **both of you earn bonus points**.

| Who | Reward |
|---|---|
| You (referrer) | **+200 pts** when your friend completes their first job |
| Your friend (referee) | **+100 pts** bonus on their first job completion |

The +100 pts your friend earns is on top of the normal job points they would earn anyway.

## Getting Your Referral Link

1. Go to **Rewards** in the sidebar
2. Find the **Invite Friends & Earn** section
3. Click **Copy** to copy your personal referral link

Your link looks like this:
```
https://localpro.ph/register?ref=ABCD1234
```

Share it anywhere — WhatsApp, Facebook, text, email.

## How Your Friend Signs Up

Your friend simply opens your link and registers normally. The referral code is captured automatically — they don't need to do anything extra.

> The referral bonus is triggered when your friend **completes their first job** (escrow is released), not just when they register.

## Tracking Your Referrals

On your **Rewards** page you can see how many friends you have successfully referred. A referral is counted once a friend has signed up using your link.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Can I refer myself or use multiple accounts?**
No — the system detects self-referrals and will not award points.

**What if my friend forgets to use my link?**
The link must be used at registration. Referral codes cannot be applied after an account is created.

**Is there a limit on how many friends I can refer?**
No limit — refer as many friends as you like and earn +200 pts for each one who completes their first job.

**When do I receive my points?**
Within seconds of your friend's first job escrow being released. You'll see the points appear in your **Rewards** activity feed.
