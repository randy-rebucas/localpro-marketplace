## What is escrow?

Escrow is a secure payment system where your money is held by a neutral third party (LocalPro) until both you and the provider agree the work is complete. This protects you from paying for work that was never done, and protects the provider from clients who refuse to pay after work is finished.

## How escrow works on LocalPro

1. You accept a provider's quote
2. You **fund the escrow** — your payment is held securely by LocalPro
3. The provider sees the escrow is funded and begins work
4. Work is completed and you confirm via the app
5. Funds are **released to the provider** automatically

Your money never goes directly to the provider until you confirm the job is done.

## When are funds released?

Funds are released in one of three ways:

- **You mark the job complete** — Once you confirm completion in the app, funds release within 24 hours.
- **Automatic release** — If you do not confirm or raise a dispute within 7 days of the provider marking the job complete, funds are released automatically to protect the provider.
- **Dispute resolution** — If you open a dispute, funds are held until our support team mediates and decides on a resolution.

## Partial releases

For larger jobs broken into milestones, you can release a portion of the escrow funds after each milestone is completed. Go to the job page and click **Release Partial Payment**, then enter the amount to release.

## What if the job isn't done correctly?

If the work is unsatisfactory:

1. Do **not** mark the job complete
2. Communicate the issue to the provider via messages
3. If unresolved, open a **dispute** from the job page
4. Our support team will review both sides and mediate a fair resolution

## Refunds

If a job is cancelled before work begins, your full escrow deposit is refunded. If work has partially started, a partial refund may be issued depending on the dispute outcome.
