## Creating a job listing

From your dashboard, click **Post a Job** in the sidebar or the prominent button on your dashboard. You'll be guided through a short form.

### Step 1 — Choose a category

Select the service category that best fits your job (e.g., Plumbing, Electrical, Cleaning). This helps providers in the right trade find your listing.

### Step 2 — Write a clear title and description

**Title examples:**
- "Fix leaking kitchen sink tap"
- "Paint 3-bedroom apartment interior"
- "Install ceiling fan in living room"

In the **description**, include:
- Exact location within your home (e.g., "master bathroom")
- Any materials already on hand
- Preferred timeline (urgent, flexible, specific date)
- Photos of the problem or space if applicable

### Step 3 — Set your budget

Enter a realistic budget range. This signals to providers what you expect to pay. If you're unsure of the cost, leave a note in the description asking for estimates — providers can still quote freely.

### Step 4 — Add your location

Enter your address or drag the map pin to your location. Your exact address is only shared with the provider you hire — other providers only see the general area.

### Step 5 — Review and publish

Read through your listing, then click **Submit Job**. Your job enters a brief validation review (usually a few minutes) before going live to providers.

## What happens after you post

- Your job appears in the provider marketplace
- Providers send quotes directly to you
- You'll receive in-app and email notifications for each quote
- You have full control over which quote to accept — there's no obligation

## Editing or closing a job

You can edit a job's description or budget while it's in **open** status. Once a quote is accepted and the job is **assigned**, edits are locked to protect both parties.

To close a job without hiring anyone, go to the job page and click **Close Job**.
