# LocalPro – PESO Partner Portal UI Structure

## 1. PESO Dashboard (Main Overview)

This is the **landing page after PESO login**.


### Dashboard Widgets

**Employment Metrics**

- Total Registered Providers

- New Providers This Month

- Active Jobs

- Jobs Completed

- Total Earnings Generated for Workers

**Workforce Metrics**

- Most In-Demand Skills

- Top Performing Providers

- Jobs per Service Category

**Local Activity**

- Jobs posted by PESO

- Providers hired via PESO

- Barangay workforce distribution

**Charts**

- Monthly Job Growth

- Provider Income Trend

- Job Category Demand

***


# 2. Workforce Registry

This is the **core database of workers in the city**.


### Page Layout

**Filters Panel**

- Service category

- Skill

- Barangay

- Certification

- Availability status

- Rating

**Worker Table**\
Columns:

- Name

- Service Category

- Skills

- Barangay

- Certification

- Rating

- Jobs Completed

- Status (Active / Inactive)

**Actions**

- View profile

- Verify provider

- Refer to job

- Send message


### Worker Profile View

Information includes:

**Basic Info**

- Name

- Contact

- Address

- Barangay

**Skills**

- Service categories

- Specializations

**Verification**

- PESO Registered

- Barangay Clearance

- Certification

**Job Performance**

- Completed jobs

- Ratings

- Income generated

***


# 3. Job Management (PESO Jobs)

This section allows PESO to **post and manage employment opportunities**.


### Job Posting Form

Fields:

- Job Title

- Service Category

- Job Description

- Location

- Number of Workers Needed

- Budget

- Job Duration

- Start Date

- Requirements

Optional tags:

- PESO Job

- LGU Project

- Community Work

- Emergency Work


### Job Management Dashboard

Tabs:

- Active Jobs

- Pending Jobs

- Completed Jobs

- Cancelled Jobs

Each job shows:

- Applicants

- Workers hired

- Job progress

- Payment status

***


# 4. PESO Referral System

Allows PESO to refer workers to jobs.


### Referral Workflow

**Step 1**\
Select worker from registry.

**Step 2**\
Select job opportunity.

**Step 3**\
Send referral notification.

Provider receives:\
"Recommended by PESO".


### UI Elements

Referral History Table:

- Worker

- Job

- Date Referred

- Status

- Result (Hired / Not Hired)

***


# 5. Training & Certification

PESO can manage training programs and worker skills.


### Training Dashboard

Shows:

- Available training programs

- Enrolled providers

- Completed training

- Certification records


### Training Form

Fields:

- Program Title

- Training Provider

- Duration

- Skills Covered

- Certification Issued

Workers can upload:

- certificates

- proof of completion

Example partners:

- Technical Education and Skills Development Authority

- Department of Labor and Employment

***


# 6. Provider Verification Panel

PESO can verify providers to increase trust.

Verification types:

- PESO Registered Worker

- LGU Resident Verified

- Certified Skilled Worker

- PESO Recommended Provider


### Verification Workflow

PESO Officer →\
Review provider documents →\
Approve verification →\
Badge added to profile.

***


# 7. Barangay Workforce Map

Visual mapping of employment activity.


### Map Dashboard

Shows:

- providers per barangay

- jobs per barangay

- active workforce clusters

- demand gaps

This helps PESO identify:

- high unemployment areas

- skill shortages

- job opportunities.

***


# 8. Livelihood Groups Management

Many PESO programs involve **community livelihood groups**.


### Group Dashboard

Shows:

- registered groups

- group members

- jobs assigned to groups

- income generated

Example groups:

- cleaning cooperatives

- construction teams

- landscaping crews


### Group Profile

Displays:

- members

- skills

- completed projects

- earnings.

***


# 9. Emergency Workforce Activation

For disasters or urgent projects.


### Emergency Job Broadcast

PESO can send **instant job alerts** to providers.

Form fields:

- Job type

- Location

- Urgency level

- Required workers

- Duration

System sends:\
Push notification to nearby providers.

***

**Workforce Reports**

# System Role Permissions

### PESO Admin

Full control of portal.


### PESO Officer

- Manage workforce

- Post jobs

- Verify providers


### PESO Viewer

- View reports only.

***


# Integration With LocalPro Core System

PESO portal connects to:

- Provider database

- Job marketplace

- Escrow payment system

- Ratings & reviews

- Client job requests

Meaning PESO sees **real employment data in real time.**
